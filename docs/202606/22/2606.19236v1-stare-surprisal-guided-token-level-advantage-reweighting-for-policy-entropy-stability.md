## 摘要
具有可验证奖励的强化学习算法（如GRPO）已成为大语言模型复杂推理的主流后训练范式，但通常会在训练过程中遭遇策略熵崩溃。我们对GRPO下令牌级熵动态进行了一阶梯度分析，并识别出一个令牌级信用分配不匹配问题：每个令牌的熵变化可分解为轨迹级优势与下一个令牌分布上的熵敏感度函数的乘积，从而形成一个优势-惊喜度四象限结构以及一个近临界性质。受此启发，我们提出了STARE（基于惊喜度引导的令牌级优势重加权以实现策略熵稳定性），该方法通过批次内惊喜度分位数识别熵关键令牌子集，选择性地重加权其有效优势，并引入目标熵闭环门以实现稳定的熵调控。在从1.5B到32B的模型规模以及三个任务族（短链思维、长链思维和多轮工具使用）上，STARE在数千步训练中保持稳定的RL训练，同时将策略熵维持在目标区间内。在AIME24和AIME25上，STARE的平均准确率比DAPO和其他竞争基线高出4%-8%，且反射令牌和响应长度同步增长，表明持续探索-利用平衡进一步释放RL训练潜力。代码可在https://github.com/hp-luo/STARE获取。

## Abstract
Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards algorithms like GRPO have emerged as the dominant post-training paradigm for complex reasoning in LLMs, yet commonly suffer from policy entropy collapse during training. We conduct a first-order gradient analysis of token-level entropy dynamics under GRPO and identify a token-level credit assignment mismatch: the per-token entropy variation decomposes into the product of the trajectory-level advantage and an entropy sensitivity function over the next-token distribution, yielding an advantage-surprisal four-quadrant structure and a near-criticality property. Motivated by it, we propose STARE (Surprisal-guided Token-level Advantage Reweighting for policy Entropy stability), which identifies entropy-critical token subsets via batch-internal surprisal quantiles, selectively reweights their effective advantages, and incorporates a target-entropy closed-loop gate for stable entropy regulation. Across model scales from 1.5B to 32B and three task families (Short CoT, Long CoT, and Multi-Turn Tool Use), STARE sustains stable RL training over thousands of steps while maintaining policy entropy within the target band. On AIME24 and AIME25, STARE outperforms DAPO and other competitive baselines by 4%-8% in average accuracy, with reflection tokens and response length growing in tandem, indicating sustained exploration-exploitation balance that further unlocks RL training potential.Code is available at https://github.com/hp-luo/STARE.
